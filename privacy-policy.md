# Privacy Policy for Matthew Explains Video Progress Saver

**Last Updated:** November 24, 2025

## Overview

Matthew Explains Video Progress Saver ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

## Data Collection

The Extension does **NOT** collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

- **No personal data is collected**: We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any other personally identifiable information
- **No tracking**: We do not track your browsing history or behavior
- **No analytics**: We do not use any analytics services or tracking tools
- **No third-party sharing**: No data is shared with third parties

## Data Storage

The Extension stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Video playback positions**: The timestamp of where you left off watching videos on matthewexplains.com pages
- **Storage location**: All data is stored using Chrome's local storage API and remains on your device
- **Storage key format**: Page paths (e.g., `/11182932/`) are used as keys to store video positions

## Data Usage

The stored video positions are used solely to:

- Resume video playback from where you left off when you return to a page
- Provide a better viewing experience on matthewexplains.com

## Data Transmission

- **No external servers**: The Extension does not communicate with any external servers
- **No network requests**: No data is sent over the internet
- **Fully offline**: All functionality works entirely within your browser

## Permissions

The Extension requests the following permission:

- **storage**: Required to save and retrieve video playback positions locally in your browser

This permission does **NOT** allow the Extension to access any data outside of what it explicitly stores.

## Data Retention

- Video positions are stored indefinitely until you manually clear them
- You can clear all stored data by:
  - Removing the Extension from Chrome
  - Clearing Chrome's extension storage data

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does **NOT** use any third-party services, including:

- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage services
- External APIs

## Affiliation

This Extension is **not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Matthew Explains** (matthewexplains.com). It is an independent tool created to enhance the viewing experience on that site.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

## Contact

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please email: info@grindsoftware.com

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Know what data is stored (only video positions for pages you visit)
- Delete all stored data (by removing the Extension or clearing Chrome storage)
- Use the Extension without providing any personal information

## Compliance

This Extension complies with:

- Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements

## Summary

**In short:** This Extension stores video playback positions locally on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.
